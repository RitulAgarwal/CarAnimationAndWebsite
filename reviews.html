Maruti Suzuki India Limited, formerly known as Maruti Udyog Limited, is an Indian automobile manufacturer, based in New Delhi. It was founded in 1981 and owned by the Government of India until 2003, when it was sold to the Japanese automaker Suzuki Motor Corporation. As of February 2022 Maruti Suzuki has a market share of 44.2 percent in the Indian passenger car market.Maruti Suzuki has two manufacturing facilities in Haryana (Gurugram and Manesar)
